# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 6, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Galactical Map**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1. Location Data
We use your location data to:
- Display the correct sky map for your current position.
- Calculate accurate satellite passes and visibility.
This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

### 2.2. Camera Usage
We request access to your device's camera for:
- Augmented Reality (AR) Sky View features.
- Capturing astrophotography images/videos if you choose to do so.
Camera data is processed in real-time and is not recorded unless you explicitly take a photo or video.

### 2.3. Motion Sensors
We use your device's accelerometer and gyroscope to determine the orientation of the device for the sky map and AR features.

## 3. Data Storage
All personal data (including location and saved favorites) is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store personal information on external servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We may use standard Apple services (like iCloud) if you choose to sync your data. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for more details.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
