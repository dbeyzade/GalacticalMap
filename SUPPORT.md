## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I use the AR Sky View?**  
A: Simply grant camera permission when prompted, then point your device at the sky. The app will automatically identify stars and planets.

**Q: Is the Satellite Tracker real-time?**  
A: Yes, we use the latest TLE data to track satellites, including the ISS, in real-time.

**Q: How do I restore my Premium purchase?**  
A: Go to the Premium screen and tap the "Restore Purchase" button at the bottom.
